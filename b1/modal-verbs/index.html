Modal Verbs

Explanation

Modal verbs (can, could, must, should, may, might, etc.) are used to express ability, possibility, necessity, advice, or permission.

  • Can – ability: I can swim.
  • Must – necessity: You must wear a seatbelt.
  • Should – advice: You should study harder.
  • May/Might – possibility: It might rain tomorrow.

Exercises

1. Write 3 sentences with can about your abilities.
2. Transform: "I am obliged to go to school" → with must.
3. Make 2 sentences with should (advice).
4. Write 2 possibilities for tomorrow using may or might.
